CAREER AND TECHNICAL EDUCATION

CUMBERLAND VALLEY HIGH SCHOOL

Cumberland Valley high school has a large variety of career pathways available for students to choose or students can create their own pathway following a set of guidelines. There are pathways available in agriculture, art, business, Family and Consumer Sciences, Music, STEM, and more. Technical education in Cumberland Valley is also offered at a vocational tech school where computer programming, construction, technology and logistics, machine technology and computer networking are taught. At Cumberland Valley high school, classes also include hands-on approach to learning and offer a wide variety from traditional classes to independent studies.

APID Diploma Program






This is a globally recognized certificate for students with an interest in international studies and for those planning to attend school outside of the United States. This program is recognized by universities worldwide. It allows those students who wish to go aboard to have additional proof of academic excellence. In combination with the high school diploma, it provides our students with a tremendous oppurtunity.



College in High School/Dual Enrollment Program



We also offer a College in High School Program which allows juniors and seniors to take college courses from the teachers in our school who are approved to teach secondary education. This does result in college credits. The Dual Enrollemnt program are taught by the university's faculty allowing students to engage in high-quality academics, build their own academic pathway, and save thousands of dollars. It earns both high school and college credits.
