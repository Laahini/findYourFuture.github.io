What is a Cluster?


A cluster is a special group collaboration session that Find Your Future has created to help rising high schoolers and college students discover their interests. In this meeting, people will get to meet people of all different fields that match their personality and career interests. Find Your Future hopes that this unique program will help several discover and learn more about new interests and passion.
