2022 Workshops

During the Find Your Future Conference, we will be offering several different opportunities for you to develop your personal skills and learn about a variety of topics regarding leadership and self-improvement from professionals in varying fields. You will also get a perspective on different careers, even having the opportunity to perform some of their daily tasks as a challenge. We want you to enjoy learning, improve yourself and start making an impact today.

What is a Cluster?


A cluster is a special group collaboration session that Find Your Future has created to help rising high schoolers and college students discover their interests. In this meeting, people will get to meet people of all different fields that match their personality and career interests. Find Your Future hopes that this unique program will help several discover and learn more about new interests and passion.
